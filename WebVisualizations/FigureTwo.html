Distribution of Nitrogen in the Galaxy

This element does not generate directly from the center of the stars. In fact, we do not know exactly how it is formed. It is thought that could formed with help from oxigen and carbon in the final stage of the evolution of an intermediate mass star (a bit higher than our Sun). The distribution shows that on the galactic plane (thin disk) and near the bulge, the distribution of nitrogen is higher since intermediate and low mass stars are abundant in this region.
