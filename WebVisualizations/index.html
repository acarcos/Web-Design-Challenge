Image taken from ESA

To learn about the evolution of our Galaxy and how all that we possess is formed, we study the distribution of star populations and the elements they possess. For this study, the Milky Way is divided by components (see Figure) such as the halo, bulge and disk (thin and thick). And here, we are going to study the elements of Oxigen (O), Nitrogen (N), Carbon (C) and Iron (Fe).
