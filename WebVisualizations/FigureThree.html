Distribution of Oxygen in the Galaxy

This element is created similar to carbon, inside the core of massive stars. But also, we see a high distribution on the thick disk and halo that is due to intermediate mass stars (mass higher than the Sun but less than eight times its mass), which also produced this element during their lives and at their final stage.
